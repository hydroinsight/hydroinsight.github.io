# HydroInsight

**HydroInsight** is a research-oriented platform focused on water resources, climate data, remote sensing, GIS, Python programming, and machine learning applications in environmental studies.

The goal of this website is to share scientific notes, research projects, tutorials, and open-source workflows related to groundwater, drought, land subsidence, GRACE data, snow depth, and hydroclimatic data analysis.

This platform is developed by **Ali Rezaee** to share reproducible research, data-driven insights, and educational materials for water and climate studies.

## Main Topics

- Water resources and groundwater analysis
- Climate data processing
- Remote sensing and GIS
- GRACE data and groundwater storage
- Land subsidence monitoring and prediction
- Drought and snow data analysis
- Python, xarray, NetCDF, and machine learning

## Research and Technical Focus

HydroInsight combines environmental science, geospatial analysis, and data science to better understand water and climate-related challenges, especially in arid and semi-arid regions.

The website will include:

- Research project summaries
- Python and GIS tutorials
- Scientific visualizations
- Technical notes
- Open-source workflows
- Links to related GitHub repositories
