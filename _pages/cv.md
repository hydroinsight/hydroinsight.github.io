## Research Profile

I am a water resources management specialist with an M.Sc. in Water Resources Management from Ferdowsi University of Mashhad. My work focuses on hydrological and climate modeling, machine learning, remote sensing, GIS, and big data analysis using Python and R.

My current research focuses on extreme hydroclimatic events, including droughts, floods, heatwaves, and compound extreme events under climate change. I am also interested in groundwater systems, land subsidence, uncertainty analysis, climate data processing, and data-driven environmental modeling.
