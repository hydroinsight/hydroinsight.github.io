## Research Profile

I am a water resources management specialist with expertise in hydrological and climate modeling, machine learning, and big data analysis using Python and R.

My current research focuses on hydroclimatic extreme events, including droughts, floods, heatwaves, and compound extreme events under climate change. I am also interested in remote sensing, GIS, groundwater systems, land subsidence, uncertainty analysis, and environmental data science.
